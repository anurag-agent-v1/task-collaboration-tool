# Idea Log - 2026-03-15

## Problem Statement
We want to keep the daily collaboration loop flowing even when one of us is busy, so the latest thoughts stay visible, the other person can review without chasing, and we always have a small, actionable idea to improve the platform.

## Idea
- Launch a “Micro-Experiment Capture” module that:
  - Lets the assistant immediately jot down one small experiment or improvement idea (just a sentence or two) whenever it arises.
  - Highlights what quick win the idea unlocks (better clarity, faster feedback, safer guardrails) and why it matters in the current collaboration streak.
  - Is stored automatically in the daily log, with a timestamp and related context so you can skim it during your review window.

## UX & Testing Focus
- Keep the entry short and scannable: a bold heading for the idea and short bullet points for why it matters and what to try.
- Add a lightweight Vitest assertion that the daily file has at least one idea with `timestamp`, `impact`, and `next-step` metadata before it’s marked ready for review.
- Consider a little helper (maybe `npm run idea-digest`) that prints the newest entry in console-friendly format so it’s easy to glance at during standups.

## New Idea: Ethical Feedback Loop

### Problem Statement
Metacollaboration ideas can drift into vague promises if we don’t close the loop on how they impact users, so we need a lightweight way to capture whether each idea stays ethical, inclusive, and aligned with your values before it’s shipped.

### Idea
- Attach an “Ethical Feedback Loop” badge to each daily entry that:
  - Asks a single question: “What’s the most important ethical consideration for this micro-experiment?”
  - Logs whether the idea is inclusive, respects user autonomy, and avoids harmful automation.
  - Offers a quick follow-up step to gather a user voice (a short survey, persona check, or accessibility review) before marking the idea ready for implementation.

### UX & Testing Focus
- Use a short form with three checkbox-style affirmations (Inclusive?, Transparent?, Safe?) so the reviewer can scan and agree quickly.
- Add a Vitest guard that fails if the badge is missing or all checkbox answers are unchecked, ensuring ethical checks are never skipped.
- Build a script (e.g., `npm run ethics-check`) that summarizes the latest badge in the console for easy review during standups.

### Next Steps
1. Record today’s ethical badge alongside the micro-experiment idea and keep collecting them in the daily log.
2. After you approve, create `ideas-generated-ai/ethical-feedback-loop` with templates for the badge, the guard tests, and guidance on collecting user voice.
3. Reflect regularly: does this badge make us pause before implementation? Adjust the prompts or evidence we collect based on your comfort level.
