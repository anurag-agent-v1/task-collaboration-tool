# Idea Log - 2026-03-17

## Problem Statement
Even though we capture ideas daily, the files lack a simple highlight of the most confidence-worthy insight so we can quickly decide what to prioritize in the next sprint.

## Idea
- Add a “Confidence Highlight” badge to the log that:
  - Summarizes the idea we feel most confident about in one sentence.
  - Gives a confidence score (low/medium/high) along with the reasoning.
  - Links to the minimal evidence (data point, user quote, trend) that justifies that confidence level.

## UX & Testing Focus
- Render the badge as a short paragraph with bold confidence and italicized evidence so it’s easy to scan, even in small mobile previews.
- Include a Vitest guard that ensures each log has exactly one Confidence Highlight before approval and that it includes score and evidence.
- Provide a helper command (`npm run highlight`) that prints the badge with a timestamp for quick standup sharing.

## Next Steps

2. After you approve, create `ideas-generated-ai/confidence-highlights` with templates, badges, and tests.
3. Reflect on whether the badge helps us spot winning ideas faster; adjust scoring or evidence prompts if it feels forced.
